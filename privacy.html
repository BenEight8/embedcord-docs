Embedcord Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Overview

Embedcord respects your privacy and only stores information necessary for the bot's functionality.

2. Information We Store

Depending on the features you use, Embedcord may store:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Discord Server IDs
  • User preferences (such as language settings)
  • Saved embeds and embed configurations
  • Bot configuration settings

3. Information We Do Not Store

Embedcord does not collect:

  • Passwords
  • Payment information
  • Private Discord messages
  • Personal information outside of what Discord provides to the bot

4. Use of Data

Stored data is used solely to:

  • Provide bot functionality
  • Save user preferences
  • Save embed configurations
  • Improve stability and performance

5. Data Sharing

Embedcord does not sell, rent or share user data with third parties.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the bot's features.

7. Data Removal

Users may request deletion of their stored data through the official Embedcord Discord server.

8. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored information. However, no method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of Embedcord after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us through the official Embedcord Discord server.
